No-AI Philosophy

Human-Curated Logic

Every combination comes from a hand-crafted database of industries, problems, and business models — not a language model. The ideas are yours to own and interpret.

Why No AI?

AI generates the expected. Structured randomness — bounded by human judgment — surfaces collisions you'd never prompt for. Serendipity is a feature, not a bug.

The Engine

Three arrays: Industries (20), Problems (20), Business Models (20). That's 8,000 unique permutations from a file you can read in five minutes.

Open Source

Fork it, extend the arrays, swap the palette. The philosophy is simple: software should be readable, auditable, and owned by its users.
